Boiled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water
  • Optional: herbs and spices (e.g., thyme, garlic)

Steps

  1. Place the chicken in a large pot.
  2. Cover with water and add salt, pepper, and any herbs/spices.
  3. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1-1.5 hours.
  5. Check the internal temperature (should be 165°F or 75°C).
  6. Remove from heat and let it rest before carving.
  7. Serve with your choice of sides.
