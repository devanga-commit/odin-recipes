Smoked Pork

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Wood chips for smoking

Instructions

  1. Mix the brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork shoulder.
  3. Wrap the pork in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

  7. Remove from the smoker and let it rest for 30 minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve with your favorite barbecue sauce.
